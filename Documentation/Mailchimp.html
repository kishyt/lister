Mailchimp Integration


1 - Create a List in Mailchimp

Open Mailchimp.com , Login and Click on Create a list .. if you are not familiarized with mailchimp check the videos.


2 - Fill your New List Name

Choose a name for your list and a reply-to email and a Description.

After Click on Save button and Your List will be created .


3 - Create a Signup Form

Now Click on "Create a Signup Form" to create and setup your form.

in this screen we are able to build and customize our form.

Customize The first Field and set the tag to "NAME"

Delete the Last Name Field ( we will create a phone field in the next step)

Create the Phone Field

Customize the Phone Field and Change the Field Tag to " PHONE " and Save.


4 - Get the Embeded Code

Click on "Lists" then Click on your List susbcribers to get the code.

After click on Signup Form Embed Code.

NOW Click on get Naked Code.

NOW Copy only the Form Action attribute .


5 - Edit the Form in Lister Template

Open index.html , and locate line 74 where the form.

Copy your Form action attribute/url

Congrats ! it's Done. You can start collecting emails ..

TIP: if you want to use more fields just add it and setup the form .
